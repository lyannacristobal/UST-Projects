About


Meet Lyanna,

a delightful 19-year-old soul born on March 22, 2004. She's currently thriving as a 2nd-year student at the prestigious University of Santo Tomas, passionately pursuing a degree in BS in Information Technology.

Lyanna resides in the heart of vibrant Sta. Ana, Manila, where the city's energy fuels her creativity. She's an artist at heart, finding solace and joy in the strokes of a paintbrush and the lines of a drawing. Her artistry is a reflection of her inner world, where imagination knows no bounds.

But there's more to Lyanna than her artistic flair. She's a devoted fan, with NCT's music serving as her personal soundtrack to life. The melodies and beats resonate with her spirit, bringing a touch of rhythm to her everyday.

One of her greatest joys is being part of a concert crowd, where music connects hearts and souls. The vibrant atmosphere, the pulsating rhythms, and the unity of fans make it an experience she treasures.

Lyanna has an adventurous side too. She's always up for trying new things, whether it's exploring exotic cuisines, discovering hidden gems in her city, or embarking on spontaneous journeys.

What sets her heart racing are superheroes from the world of DC Comics and Marvel. Batman, The Flash, Robin, Bucky Barnes, and Captain America are her ultimate favorites. Their tales of heroism inspire her and remind her that anyone can be a hero in their own way.

Lyanna's journey is a tapestry woven with diverse interests, passions, and an unwavering spirit of exploration. Her story is still unfolding, and she's excited to share it with the world.
